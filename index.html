Start
-Press the J and K keys alternately to move Dan the Man up.
-A and D to move left or right, W to shoot.
-Shoot Doctor Duck's ship to knock him out.
-When he's knocked out ,land on Doctor Duck's head to score 1 point( When there's a red arrow).
-Don't encounter Doctor Duck's ship when he's awake, you'll be hurt.
-Shoot the meteors ( ) when you reach 5 points to find ammunition supplies.

-Remember to save your ammo.
-The only goal is to score as many points as you can. Good luck. (Mobile devices will be supported soon!)
